Welcome to ginmappeR

ginmappeR provides functionalities to translate gene or protein identifiers between state-of-art biological sequence databases: CARD, NCBI Protein, Nucleotide and Gene, UniProt and KEGG.

With ginmappeR, you can easily map identifiers across different databases, ensuring that you always have the most accurate and up-to-date information. In addition to identifier mapping, ginmappeR also offers complementary functionality like NCBI identical proteins or UniProt similar genes clusters retrieval.

Whether you're a researcher, a bioinformatician, or just interested in gene and protein data, ginmappeR is the tool for you.
